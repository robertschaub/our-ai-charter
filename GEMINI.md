# GEMINI.md — Our AI Charter

The authoritative working rules for this repository are in **[AGENTS.md](AGENTS.md)** — read it first and follow it. Gemini does not read `AGENTS.md` automatically, so the essentials:

- **This is a PUBLIC, documents-only repo** (manifesto + charter + draft protocol). No code, build, or tests; don't add them.
- **Route new files by content** (see AGENTS.md → *Where new files go*): public charter/published material here; legal, finance, outreach, contacts, personal data, and strategy go to the private sibling `FactHarbor-internal`. Unsure → private, and ask.
- **Never** put confidential, personal, or not-yet-public content here, and **never commit or push without maintainer review**.
- As a maintainer-side agent, you may read/write the private sibling only when the task requires it and your workspace grants access (e.g. launched with `--include-directories`); never copy its confidential content into this public repo.
